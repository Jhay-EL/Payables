## Features

- **Unified Dashboard**  
  View your total upcoming outgoings, updated in real time, broken down by currency—all at a glance.  

- **Upcoming Payments List**  
  Colorful, icon‑driven cards show each upcoming charge (Spotify, Netflix, utilities, and more) with due dates and amounts.  

- **Smart Filters & Categories**  
  Filter by time frame (“This Week”, “This Month”) or custom categories (Entertainment, Cloud Storage, Insurance, Utilities, etc.) to focus on what matters most.  

- **Insights & Spend Analysis**  
  Visualize spending with bar charts. Compare categories, spot trends, and optimize your budget each month.  

- **Customizable Notifications**  
  Never miss a due date—set reminders for each bill or subscription and personalize when and how you receive alerts.  

- **Multi‑Currency Support**  
  Automatically convert and display amounts in your preferred currency—ideal for global travelers and expatriates.  

- **Appearance & Widget Options**  
  Switch between light/dark modes, choose what to show on your home‑screen widget, and tailor the look and feel to suit your style.  

- **Backup & Restore**  
  Securely export data to Excel and restore backups at any time, ensuring your financial history is always safe.  

- **Privacy & Control**  
  Keep your data private—erase all stored information with a single tap or review our transparent privacy policy under “About Payables.”
